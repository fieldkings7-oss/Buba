### Plants Implemented

   - HP: 100
   - Damage: 15 per shot
   - Cooldown: 1.5 seconds

2. **Cherry Bomb**
   - Cost: 150 Sun
   - HP: 150
   - Damage: 300 (explosion)
   - Range: 300 pixels

3. **Hybrid Pea (Premium)**
   - Cost: 100 Sun (after 500 coin purchase)
   - HP: 100
   - Damage: 50 per shot
   - Cooldown: 1.2 seconds
   - Explosion Range: 150 pixels

### Zombies Implemented
1. **Space Astronaut Zombie**
   - HP: 100
   - Speed: 50 pixels/sec
   - Attack: 10 damage/sec (eating plants)
   - Spawn Rate: Every 3 seconds
   - Max per Level: 8

### Currency Systems

**Sun (In-Game)**
- Start: 200 per level
- Cost: Plant purchases
- Earned from: Defeated zombies
- Reset: Each new level

**Coins (Persistent)**
- Cost: Unlock upgrades (500 for Hybrid)
- Earned: 100 + (50 Ã— level) per victory
- Saved To: `user://pvz_save.json`
- Resets: Never
